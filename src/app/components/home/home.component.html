Step 1: Find Your ESPN League ID

Navigate to your league's home page.

Located in the URL you will find the League ID Value & Associated SeasonID: "leagueId=XXXXXX&seasonId=1234"

Step 2: Locate SWID & ESPN S2 Cookies

Visit your league's homepage.

Right-click and select "Inspect."

Go to 'Application.'

Under 'Cookies,' select 'https://fantasy.espn.com.'

Look for 'SWID' and 'ESPN_S2' variables on the right.
